# Shipley Milestone 4 Pricing Review

## Purpose

Support pricing review discussions with a concise evidence-based summary.

## Default workflow

1. Review available pricing and award context.
2. Identify affordability, competitiveness, and pricing risk signals.
3. Separate observed evidence from pricing inference.
4. State the review outcome and open gaps.

## Output

Return the pricing review summary, evidence, confidence, and follow-up questions.

# Subaward Flags

## Purpose

Surface subaward patterns that may affect teaming strategy, incumbent assessment, or risk.

## Default workflow

1. Gather subaward and related award context.
2. Identify concentration, repetition, or unusual patterns.
3. Distinguish verified data from interpretation.
4. Report actionable flags and unresolved questions.

## Output

Return the observed pattern, why it matters, supporting evidence, confidence, and open gaps.

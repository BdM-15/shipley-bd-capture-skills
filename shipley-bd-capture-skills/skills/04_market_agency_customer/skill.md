# Market Agency Customer Analysis

## Purpose

Frame the market and customer environment around an opportunity using reusable upstream research.

## Default workflow

1. Pull relevant agency, market, and customer signals.
2. Normalize the findings into a capture-ready summary.
3. Separate direct evidence from analytical inference.
4. Highlight actionable implications and PRIME gaps.

## Output

Return market context, customer signals, evidence, confidence, and actions to investigate next.
